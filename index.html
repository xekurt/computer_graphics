  • Animation: 360-degree camera orbit parented to an "Empty" anchor.

Obstacles Encountered

The "Void" Problem

Objects appeared to float; solved by grounding geometry.

Light Bleeding

Tweaked Eevee‚Äôs "Contact Shadows" and "Shadow Map" settings.

Scale Proportions

Balanced chair and desk dimensions for realistic feel.

The biggest frustration was the "gray void" where objects looked ungrounded. I solved this through technical render settings and ensuring crisp edges on the low-poly geometry using Eevee's shadow maps.
